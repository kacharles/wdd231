Website Planning Document

Site Name

The site will be called BYU-PW Application Guide. The site will be hosted on github pages via the following url: https://kacharles.github.io/wdd231/final-project/

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is the help new and potential student apply to BYU-Pathway Worlwide and easily register for their classes by streamlining the application and registration process.

Target Audience

High School students seeking higher education and some university students.

Scenarios

  • How does one apply to BYU-Pathway?
  • How do I register for classes?
  • What are certificates and degrees offered through BYU-Pathway?
